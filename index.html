Who we are

Capio is a platform dedicated to connecting college students to trendy and fashionable luxury wear. At the click of a button on your smartphone, gain access to a world of new clothes for rent - at unbelievably low costs and sourced entirely from your peers.

Trendy clothes when you need them - that's Capio.

Our Mission

As the famous designer, Rachel Zoe, once said, “Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak.” By facilitating a collective closet, we at Capio hope to foster campus cultures of shared style and accessibility across the US. In doing so, we aim to open up innumerable doors long locked to those on limited budgets, and bring Rachel's vision for fashion to all.

Our mission is to unify people - together, we make a collective community, a collective mind, and a collective closet.
