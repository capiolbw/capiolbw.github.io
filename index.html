Our Mission

Capio is a platform dedicated to connecting college students to trendy and fashionable luxury wear within their respective campuses. Through a collective closet, students will foster a campus culture of shared style and accessibility. (collective smart shoppers)

Our mission is to unify communities - together we are a collective community, a collective mind, and a collective closet.

Get started with just four easy steps

  • Explore Our Closet:

    1. Search the app for styles that fit
    2. Meet your renter; receive clothes
    3. Return clothes & confirm quality
    4. Enjoy the benefits!

  • Share Your Style:

    1. List your clothing on the app
    2. Meet your rentee; give clothes
    3. Receive clothes & confirm quality
    4. Enjoy the benefits!
